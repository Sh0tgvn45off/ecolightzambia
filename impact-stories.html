Introduction of EcoLight Innovation Zambia

It is an initiative dedicated to enhancing climate resilience in Zambia by bridging the energy access gap to marginalized communities and improving their overall living standards through empowering them with affordable, reliable, renewable, and eco-friendly solar lighting energy sources, running outreach programs in Schools and Communities on Climate Change Education and Awareness Campaigns, and conducting capacity building workshops.
